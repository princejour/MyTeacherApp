# Privacy Policy — My Teacher

Effective date: July 14, 2026

This policy applies to **My Teacher**, Android package `com.walhero.myteacher`.

Public policy URL:

`https://princejour.github.io/my-teacher-privacy.html`

## School communication information

The app is designed for communication between teachers and families. It may display student names, class information, family access codes, teacher passcodes, and teacher messages.

In the current version, this information is processed locally inside the app. The current source code does not upload teacher messages, family codes, student names, or teacher passcodes to a developer-operated server. Current demo state may be removed when the app closes, its storage is cleared, or the app is uninstalled.

If Firebase, cloud synchronization, accounts, notifications, or another server-based feature is added later, this policy and the store Data Safety declaration must be updated before release.

## Google AdMob

The app uses Google AdMob rewarded ads after the monthly free-message limit. A locked message unlocks only after the rewarded-ad completion callback is received.

Depending on region, device settings, and consent choices, Google and its advertising partners may process IP address, approximate location derived from IP, device or advertising identifiers, ad interactions, app activity related to ad delivery, diagnostics, security, and fraud-prevention information.

Google processes this information under its own policies:

- Google Privacy Policy: `https://policies.google.com/privacy`
- Google advertising technologies: `https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads`

## Consent and privacy choices

The app integrates Google User Messaging Platform (UMP). It requests updated consent information at app launch, shows a Google-certified consent form when required, and does not request ads until UMP reports that ads may be requested.

When required, a visible **Privacy choices** option lets the user review or change advertising consent. The app also provides direct access to the public Privacy Policy.

## Permissions

The app uses internet and network-state access to load AdMob advertisements and privacy messages. The current version does not request contacts, microphone, camera, precise location, SMS, or phone permissions.

## Data deletion

Current locally processed app state can be removed by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app. Information independently processed by Google AdMob is retained according to Google's policies and the user's applicable consent choices.

## Children and family use

The app is intended to be operated by teachers, parents, and guardians for school communication. Adults should avoid entering information that is not necessary for school communication.
